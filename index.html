How To Rent

Step 1

Get What You Need

Browse over 100 products from 40+ brands. Select the gear you need for the nights you plan to use your gear to see the price for your trip. Get excited for your trip!

Step 2

Go Advanture!

Head outside and enjoy your gear! Questions about how to use your gear? Contact us! Our team is full of experts who can walk you through any questions you may have.

Step 3

Return Your Gear

When you are done with your gear, simply put it back in the box and adhere the free return label. Drop it off at any FedEx location. You can also drop it off at any Walgreens location that is a participating FedEx partner.

About Us
